Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our car transport services in Delhi

How can I book car transport services with 4 Wheeler Movers?

Booking with us is simple ‚Äî you can fill out our online inquiry form, call our customer support, or visit our Delhi office for personalized assistance.

How long does it take to transport a car across India?

The delivery timeline depends on the distance and route. We strive to provide an estimated delivery time frame during the booking process.

Is my vehicle insured during transit?

Yes, we provide transit insurance to ensure your vehicle is fully protected throughout the journey.

What types of vehicles can you transport?

We specialize in transporting all types of vehicles, including hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, luxury cars, and even two-wheelers.

What documents are required for car transportation?

You will need to provide a copy of your vehicle's RC (Registration Certificate), insurance papers, and a valid government-issued ID.

How is my car transported securely?

We use specially designed car carriers equipped with safety features like wheel locks, straps, and secure fastening systems.

Can I track my vehicle during transit?

Yes, we offer real-time tracking services to keep you informed about your vehicle's location and estimated delivery time.

Do I need to prepare my car before transportation?

Yes, we recommend cleaning your vehicle, removing personal belongings, checking for leaks, and ensuring the fuel tank is at a minimum level (usually a quarter tank).

What factors affect car transportation costs?

The cost depends on factors like distance, vehicle size, carrier type (open or enclosed), and additional services such as doorstep delivery.

Do you provide doorstep pickup and delivery?

Yes, we offer convenient doorstep pickup and delivery services across India for your comfort and ease.

Are there any items I should avoid leaving in my car during transport?

Yes, please remove all personal belongings, valuables, electronic devices, and important documents. Also, remove any custom accessories that can be detached.

How do I pay for car transport services?

We accept various payment methods including online transfers, credit/debit cards, and cash. A deposit is typically required at booking, with the balance due upon delivery.

What happens if my car is damaged during transport?

In the rare event of damage, our comprehensive transit insurance covers repairs. We conduct thorough inspections before and after transport to document the vehicle's condition.

Can you transport non-operational vehicles?

Yes, we can transport non-operational vehicles, but please inform us in advance as special equipment may be required, which could affect pricing.

Do you offer express or expedited shipping?

Yes, we offer express shipping options for customers who need their vehicles delivered on priority. Additional charges apply for this premium service.
